Multimedia Artist and Educator

Binh Ngo Dai Chien - BNDC (2020). I joined the team Viet Su Kieu Hung (VSKH) as an animator to make the movie BINH NGO DAI CHIEN. VSKH is a non-profit project with the mission to enhance the young generation's interest in learning about Vietnamese history through animated series portraying prominent figures, battles, and events. The most ambitious part of the project, the full-length 60-minute animation BINH NGO DAI CAO successfully raised 1-billion-Vietnam-Dong (~50,000 USD) for the production process.

After premiering on YouTube on December 22, 2020, the animation instantly gained overwhelming attention with more than 3.6M views and stayed among the top trending videos in Vietnam.
