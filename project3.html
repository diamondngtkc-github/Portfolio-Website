BINH NGO DAI CHIEN | VSKH

The movie BINH NGO DAI CHIEN is the latest part of the larger project by Viet Su Kieu Hung (VSKH). VSKH is a non-profit project with the mission of inspiring Vietnamese history to the Vietnamese people. The project is carried out in the form of a historical film in the style of animation, recreating historical figures, battles, or important events in Vietnamese history. The most ambitious part of the project, the full-length animation BINH NGO DAI CAO, will hit the big screen in the late 2020 after successfully raise more than 1 billion Vietnam Dong (~50,000 USD) for the production process.

Role: Animator

Team: Viet Su Kieu Hung - VSKH
