Van Gogh AI | Prototype

As I am big on Van Gogh's artworks, I would want people to get to know more about his life and his masterpieces. Through this self-project, I want to make a virtual assistant similar to Siri to answer questions about Vincent van Gogh. You can simply ask with your voice and the machine will answer. Within 50 lines of code, it is cool to have a natural short conversation with the machine. I would like to incorporate more external libraries to expand the scope of questions that people can ask without having to hard code all the answers. (2020)
