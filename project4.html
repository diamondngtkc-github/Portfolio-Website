MANMADE

ManMade is an underwater VFX short film which combines both live action footage and 3D character animation. The film is about a journey of a young boy into the deep water to explore the adaption of sea creatures in response to pollution and environmental changes. This is the first underwater VFX short film that is produced by ADM students. The project was selected to be featured at the Sunday Showcase at ArtScience Museum, Singapore on May 23, 2015. We are only a group of two to produce this short film. I am in charge of creating all the assets and environments like characters, fishes, coral reefs, rocks and cliffs, etc. After that, I also did all the rigging, animation before handling over to my group mate doing the post-production and VFX.

Many thanks to our project supervisor Bejamin Seide, our Animation faculty, our beloved Animation friends, our talented cast Rosha Chandra - the actor, Daniel Chong - the D.O.P, Tobias Hoffmann - the music composer and Jared - the diver.

Role: Producer, Director, Animator
