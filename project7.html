sccc (2020). I produced an infographics video for the Singapore Chinese Culture Center (SCCC) about the etiquettes that people need to follow in the gallery. There were three main sections in the video: Care and Share, Be Courteous and Show Kindness. Through the lens of three main characters in the story, the audiences would understand how to behave within the premise of SCCC.

Medium: 2D Animation

Role: Concept, Design and Animator

Agency: Sproud
