Ponderwonderawedito | OUE Downtown2

We built a visual animated spectacle 'Ponderwonderaweditto' which was designed around a circus theme showcasing ancient Chinese scripts at atouch, an animated circus wonderland projected over 32x12 metres with never the same animation, and the ability toattract or disperse over 30-thousand butterflies by simply waving your hand.

The visual-interactive experience can be perceived from either exterior or interior, ‘Ponderwonderaweditto’ is one of the world’s largest interactive permanent projections, and gives a dynamic brand direction for OUE Downtown Gallery

Role: Concept, Production, Lead Visual Artist, Assistant Programmer

Agency: IcebergX
