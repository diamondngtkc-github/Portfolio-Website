Zen pond | Prototype

2020 is a demanding year for everyone inevery aspect. To balance life, I often seekpeace in meditation and enjoy feeding myfishes in the pond. This interactive piece isa prototype for a large-scale installationthat I envision. TouchOSC on the phone isused in place of motion detector sensors.In the real installation, users can walk on aprojected area or a big screen. Every stepgets converted into ripples within this zenpond as if people are walking on the water.They can enjoy the peacefulness of thepond with fishes swimming freely andfloating lotus leaves. The colors of thepond also vary throughout the experienceas if it reacts to the sunlight. The wholeexperience aims to deliver a calmingsensation for users and give them a senseof zen. (2020)

Role: Concept Artist & Programmer
