LIVING SPACE | HDB Gallery

One of the largest interactive project that I did in IcebergX. We revamped the whole Housing Development Board (HDB) gallery which was outdated with latest innovative technology. The project was a huge success by growing the number of visitors to the new gallery by 200%. There are several sections including Journey, Plan, Eco, Create, Share, and Skyview. The exhibition utilizes a variety of interactive and immersive techniques: movement sensors, touch-screen, projection, projection mapping.

Role: Concept Design, Production, Lead Visual Artist

Agency: IcebergX

KEY VISUALS

PLAN: Interactive projection about how HDB plans the housing development

CREATE: Interactive Touch-screen gamified content about Housing Policies

ECO: Interactive Touch-screen content about SMART TOWN

SHARE: Interactive projection about community across estates in Singapore
