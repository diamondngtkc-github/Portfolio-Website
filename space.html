SPACE | Animated short

Things grow big, but space is limited.

SPACE is a short 2D animation about the relationship between human versus nature. While the human figure is represented in the form of a fetus, the natural force is symbolized as a growing bean. They are both growing and yet struggling to share the same space. Who will win?. (2014)

Role: Concept & Production
